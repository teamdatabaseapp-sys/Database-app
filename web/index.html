PLATFORM

One platform.
Every tool you need.

Multi-Store Support
Manage multiple locations from a single account. Each store has independent staff, services, calendars, and reporting — with unified owner-level visibility.
Staff Roles & Permissions
Structured access for every team member. Owner sees everything. Managers handle operations. Staff see only their own schedule and clients. Clean and secure.
Gift Cards
Create branded digital gift cards your clients can purchase and share instantly. Perfect for holidays, referrals, and driving first-time visits.

Reward clients automatically. Configure point multipliers, reward thresholds, and tier perks. Loyalty data syncs across all your locations in real time.

Build recurring revenue with customizable membership plans. Set billing cycles, included services, and exclusive member pricing — all managed in-app.
Brand Customization
Your logo, your colors, your identity. Database adapts to your brand across your booking page, emails, and client-facing touchpoints.
